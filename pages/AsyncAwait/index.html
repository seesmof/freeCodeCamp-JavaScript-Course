Async Await

Okay, let us do a little bit of research on our own this time, just to tryna get a grasp of what async/await is all about. So, as I understand it right now, async is a way to declare an asynchronous function, which always returns a promise. And await is a way to wait for the promise to resolve. And the interesting part is that those functions look and work as if they were synchronous. So in async function we wait for each event to finish before continuing the execution, I already checked that. Now lets find out a bit more from the video. Oh, hold up. A few small details. You can only use await keyword in the async functions. When such function is called, it is executed in the background and the program flow is immediately returned to the caller place.
